Friends is a popular comedy series that aired from 1994 to 2004, following the lives of six friends.

Characters

Rachel Green
Fashion enthusiast and independent.
Ross Geller
Paleontologist, romantic, and funny.
Monica Geller
Obsessed with cleanliness, an amazing chef.
Joey Tribbiani
Aspiring actor, known for his charm and love for food.
Chandler Bing
Witty and sarcastic, known for his humor and awkwardness.
Phoebe Buffay
Eccentric musician, known for her quirky personality and songs.
